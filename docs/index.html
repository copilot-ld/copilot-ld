Why

Traditional RAG systems treat knowledge as plain text, leading to responses based on text similarity rather than actual meaning. Copilot-LD is different: it uses structured linked data to understand relationships and context, delivering responses grounded in your knowledge graph‚Äînot just matching keywords.
