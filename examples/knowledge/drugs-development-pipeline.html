Oncora

A first-line targeted oncology therapy utilizing selective inhibition of aberrant kinase signaling pathways to reduce proliferative tumor activity. Mechanism centers on dual-site occupancy reducing resistance emergence. Formulation is oral, immediate-release with optimized bioavailability profile.

Platforms & Data Dependencies

Oncora-XR

Extended-release oncology formulation providing sustained plasma concentrations, reducing peak-trough variability and enhancing adherence in multi-cycle regimens.

Formulation & Compliance Platforms

Cardiozen

Cardiovascular therapy modulating endothelial nitric oxide synthesis and arterial elasticity, targeting multi-factorial hypertension profiles. Oral capsule with controlled dissolution coating.

Manufacturing Platforms

Immunex

Immunomodulatory biologic enhancing adaptive T-cell activation thresholds, supporting durable remission profiles in autoimmune conditions. Subcutaneous injection with stabilized protein folding excipients.

Analytics Platforms

Immunex-Plus

Enhanced formulation of Immunex with adjunct receptor co-stimulation profile enabling broader patient stratification responsiveness.

Development Platforms

Neurova

Neurological therapy modulating synaptic plasticity via selective regulation of glutamatergic signaling attenuation and neurotrophic support pathways. Oral modified-release tablet.

Monitoring Platforms

Vitalis

Supportive care formulation enhancing metabolic resilience and systemic recovery markers during multi-modal oncology regimens. Oral granular powder mix.

Lifecycle Platforms

NovaMed

Novel small-molecule therapeutic targeting inflammatory cascade modulation through selective cytokine pathway inhibition. Designed for chronic inflammatory conditions with optimized oral bioavailability and sustained tissue penetration profile. Formulation includes gastro-resistant coating for targeted intestinal release.

Discovery & Safety Platforms

OncoVex

Next-generation oncolytic immunotherapy combining viral vector-mediated tumor lysis with immune checkpoint modulation. Engineered virus selectively replicates in tumor cells while expressing immune-stimulatory transgenes. Administered via intratumoral injection with expansion to systemic immune response.

Biologic Development Platforms

NeuroGenex

Neuroprotective agent leveraging novel mitochondrial biogenesis pathway activation to enhance neuronal resilience and reduce oxidative stress in neurodegenerative conditions. Combines blood-brain barrier penetration with sustained CNS exposure via lipid nanoparticle formulation.
