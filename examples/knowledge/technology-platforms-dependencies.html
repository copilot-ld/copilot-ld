BioNova Technology Platform Dependency Graph

This document defines fourteen platforms as SoftwareApplication items with explicit dependency relationships (softwareRequirements and data-rel="dependsOn" links), cross-functional project associations, product/drug context, and event anchors. Rich descriptions enhance vector retrieval while structured links power graph queries.

DataLake

Centralized object and columnar storage layer aggregating clinical trial records, manufacturing telemetry, analytical feature sets, and compliance logs. Provides schema evolution, partition optimization, and lineage tracking.

MolecularForge

Computational chemistry and molecular property prediction environment integrating structure-based docking, multi-target scoring, and resistance pathway simulations.

BioAnalyzer

High-throughput assay ingestion and feature extraction platform performing normalization, quality flagging, and multimodal fusion for downstream predictive modeling.

DrugDesignStudio

Interactive compound ideation suite combining generative chemistry models, ADMET prediction, and synthetic accessibility scoring.

ClinicalStream

Streaming ingestion and harmonization pipeline converting heterogeneous clinical trial source data into standardized longitudinal datasets with real-time schema compliance enforcement.

ProcessControl

Real-time manufacturing orchestration capturing equipment telemetry, applying deviation detection models, and publishing corrective action alerts.

ManufacturingOS

Unified manufacturing execution backbone integrating batch scheduling, quality checkpoints, and supply chain visibility.

MLflow

Experiment tracking and model lifecycle management layer capturing parameters, metrics, artifacts, and deployment lineage for predictive pipelines.

AnalyticsHub

Unified analytical workspace providing semantic feature catalogs, dashboard composition, and outcome correlation exploration.

CloudOps

Provisioning and orchestration layer supplying scalable container runtime, secrets injection framework, and multi-zone deployment automation.

APIGateway

Unified service exposure and request mediation layer handling routing, rate limiting, version negotiation, and audit tagging.

SecurityVault

Centralized cryptographic secrets and key management platform enforcing rotation policies, envelope encryption, and granular access governance.

ComplianceMonitor

Regulatory intelligence and continuous compliance scoring engine aggregating policy changes, audit artifacts, and data integrity validations.

PharmaSync

Commercial access orchestration platform synthesizing therapeutic positioning, payer model outcomes, and integrated objection handling briefs.
