Team Lead

Responsible for technical direction, team coordination, performance management, and project delivery. Reports directly to division director. Oversees 4-8 engineers.

Senior Engineer

Expert-level technical contributor with deep domain expertise. Mentors junior engineers, leads technical initiatives, and drives architectural decisions. Reports to team lead.

Engineer

Mid-level technical contributor capable of independent project execution. Contributes to design decisions and supports team initiatives. Reports to team lead.

Junior Engineer

Early-career technical contributor learning core competencies. Executes well-defined tasks under guidance of senior team members. Reports to team lead.

Division Director

Executive leader responsible for division strategy, operations, and business outcomes. Manages team leads and sets technical direction. Reports to CTO or CEO.
